Product Overview

CopiousLMS is powered by the K.I.N.G. Framework to govern every piece of workplace training and documentation.

The Three Modules

Learn

Scenario-based AI training that adapts to real job conditions. Evaluate, cite, and certify automatically.

Create

Generate and version safety policies, SOPs, and toolbox talks in minutes. Every revision is audit-traceable.

Connect

Send bulletins, incidents, and corrective actions directly to teams, with acknowledgment tracking.

BYOLLM Integration

Use Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, or your own private LLM. CopiousLMS enforces safety and compliance protocols in all cases.
